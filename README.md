# Dhaya-Sethu - A Web App to keep the records for ari procurements and payments digitally in a centralized place.

- Buyers Who registered with us, and record their procurements on each farmer, and finalize them based on the market rates & transaportation charges, then handle the payments for purchases and can view their transactions.

- Farmers, while selling their products, buyers find that farmer(either through the name/mobile/qr code) based on the search results, add the procurements under that farmer by entering crop, units(fetched from the system), quantity.

- Farmers can keep track of their pending & finalized agri purchases, and can view their payment balances of each crop under each buyer and see their transactions.
